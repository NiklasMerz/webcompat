## What it does

Tracks web feature support inside WebView environments — Android WebView, WKWebView on iOS, and others. WebViews often lag behind their corresponding browser engines and have their own quirks, making this a distinct compatibility surface from the main browser targets.

## Who is it for

Developers building hybrid mobile apps (Cordova, Capacitor, React Native WebView) or mini-apps where content runs inside an embedded WebView rather than a full browser.
