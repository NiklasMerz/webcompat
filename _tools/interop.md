## What it does

Interop is an annual cross-browser effort that aligns browser vendors on a shared set of focus areas and scores progress using web-platform-tests. It helps teams prioritize work on the same interoperability gaps and make year-over-year progress visible.

## Who is it for

Browser engineering teams, standards contributors, and developer-relations teams who need a common target for improving compatibility across engines.

## Where to find it

The project overview and yearly focus areas are published at interop.dev, and implementation progress can be tracked through linked test and dashboard resources.

## Who is maintaining it

Interop is coordinated by major browser vendors and ecosystem partners, with collaboration across standards and testing communities.
