## What it does

Tests how assistive technologies (screen readers, braille displays) respond to ARIA patterns across different browser and AT combinations. Generates reports that show whether a given ARIA authoring practice produces the expected announcements in real AT.

## Who is it for

AT developers and browser vendors validating ARIA implementation correctness, accessibility spec authors, and developers who want to understand the real-world AT behaviour of specific ARIA patterns.

## Where to find it

[aria-at.w3.org/reports](https://aria-at.w3.org/reports)

The W3C ARIA-AT Community Group, with participation from AT vendors and browser implementors.
