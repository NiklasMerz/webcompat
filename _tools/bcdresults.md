## What it does

Stores raw test results collected by BCD Collector from real browsers. Each entry records which tests passed or failed in a specific browser version, providing the evidence base for updating entries in `mdn/browser-compat-data`.

## Who is it for

BCD contributors who need evidence when updating or adding compat entries, and researchers analysing historical compat data across browser versions.

## Where to find it

[github.com/openwebdocs/mdn-bcd-results](https://github.com/openwebdocs/mdn-bcd-results)

Results are collected periodically as new browser versions ship.

## Who is maintaining it

Open Web Docs. The results are generated automatically by the BCD Collector tool running against real browsers.
