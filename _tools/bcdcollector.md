## What it does

Runs a large suite of feature-detection tests against a real browser and reports which APIs and properties are available. The output feeds directly into `mdn/browser-compat-data` as evidence for adding or updating compat entries.

## Who is it for

BCD contributors who want to verify compat data in a specific browser version — especially useful when a new browser version ships or when coverage of a lesser-tested browser is needed.
