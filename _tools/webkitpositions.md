## What it does

A GitHub repository where the WebKit team records Apple's formal position on web standards proposals from W3C, WHATWG, TC39, and other bodies. Positions — "support", "neutral", "oppose", or "under consideration" — are filed as GitHub issues and accompanied by written rationale. The repository is the authoritative place to check WebKit's stance before or during a feature's standardisation.

## Who is it for

Standards editors and proposal authors seeking signal from Apple, web developers trying to understand whether Safari is likely to implement a feature, and anyone monitoring multi-vendor agreement around a proposed web capability.

## Where to find it

[github.com/WebKit/standards-positions](https://github.com/WebKit/standards-positions)

## Who is maintaining it

The WebKit team at Apple, with positions reviewed by engineers and program managers involved in web standards work.
