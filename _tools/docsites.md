## What it does

Documentation and reference sites that surface browser compatibility information to developers. They consume raw compat data (primarily from BCD and Web Features) and present it in human-readable form — tables, dashboards, search interfaces.

## Who is it for

Web developers looking up whether a specific API, property, or feature is supported in the browsers they need to target.

## Where to find it

Individual sites in this category: MDN, CanIUse, CanIEmail, CanIWebview.

## Who is maintaining it

Each site is independently maintained — see the individual entries for details.
